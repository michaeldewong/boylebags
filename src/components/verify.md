## 4. Ideal B2B Homepage Flow (Manufacturer)

**Section order:** Hero → Use Cases → Product Categories → Proof / Capabilities → CTA

| Order | Section | Section title (suggested) | One CTA (per section) | Where buyer clicks next |
|-------|---------|---------------------------|------------------------|--------------------------|
| 1 | **Hero** | *(no extra title; H1 is the headline)* | **Start High-Volume Quote** | `/custom` — main RFQ form |
| 2 | **Use Cases** | Where Your Volume Orders Go to Work | **Get a Quote for My Use Case** (or per-row: “Get Quote” per use case) | `/custom` with intent; or scroll to Product Categories |
| 3 | **Product Categories** | Tote Bags by Application | **View Specs & Quote** (per category) | `/shop` or `/shop/[category]` → then PDP → `/custom` |
| 4 | **Proof / Capabilities** | Why Buyers Choose Us / Capabilities | **Request Sample Pack** or **Start High-Volume Quote** | `/sample-pack` or `/custom` |
| 5 | **CTA** | Ready to Order 500+ Units? | **Get Tiered Quote (MOQ Applies)** | `/custom` |

### Flow summary

1. **Hero** — Establish manufacturer value (MOQ, timeline, factory-direct). Single primary CTA to RFQ.  
   **Next click:** Start High-Volume Quote → `/custom`.

2. **Use Cases** — Match visitor’s job (events, corporate, retail, nonprofit, education). One CTA for the section or one per use case.  
   **Next click:** Get a Quote (or “Get Quote” on their use case) → `/custom` (optionally with use case pre-selected).

3. **Product Categories** — Show what you make (event, corporate, retail, nonprofit, education).  
   **Next click:** View Specs & Quote → `/shop` → choose product → PDP → Continue to Quote Request → `/custom`.

4. **Proof / Capabilities** — Trust (on-time %, quality check, mockups, logistics).  
   **Next click:** Request Sample Pack → `/sample-pack`, or Start High-Volume Quote → `/custom`.

5. **CTA** — Final gate: 500+ only, no retail.  
   **Next click:** Get Tiered Quote (MOQ Applies) → `/custom`.
